Our Story

Pioneers of the expat community on Koh Samui

Our story on the island began in 2009 as a travel agency and information portal for independent tourists. We became the founders of the expat community on Koh Samui, and what started as a small kindergarten has grown into a complete educational ecosystem and villa community.

Today our diverse work spans a kindergarten, a Russian program school, an international school, and a premium villa community — everything a family needs to build a fulfilling life on Samui.
